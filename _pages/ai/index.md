## Hermes Agent

An open-source multi-profile AI agent by Nous Research. Runs locally, orchestrates tools, manages persistent memory, and operates 24/7 via cron jobs.

This section documents the enhancements, configurations, and integrations we've built on top of Hermes since May 2026.
