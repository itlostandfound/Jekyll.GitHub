## F5 iRules

TCL-based event-driven scripting inside the data plane. The Swiss army knife of BIG-IP — and the easiest way to break production.

### Key Topics

- **Events** — CLIENT_ACCEPTED, HTTP_REQUEST, HTTP_RESPONSE, LB_SELECTED
- **Commands** — pool, virtual, redirect, respond, drop, log
- **Variables** — Global vs. session vs. connection scope
- **Performance** — OneConnect, iRules optimization, CPU cost
- **SSL** — Handshake manipulation, SNI routing, cert inspection
- **HTTP** — Header manipulation, URI rewriting, content replacement
- **Gotchas** — Infinite loops, variable scoping, priority ordering
