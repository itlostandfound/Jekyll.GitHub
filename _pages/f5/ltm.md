## F5 LTM — Local Traffic Manager

The core of the BIG-IP platform. LTM handles inbound traffic, distributes it across pools, and keeps applications available.

### Key Topics

- **Virtual Servers** — IP:port listeners, profiles, and traffic policies
- **Pools & Members** — Health monitors, load balancing methods, priority groups
- **Profiles** — TCP, SSL, HTTP, OneConnect, and the dozens of knobs
- **SNAT & NAT** — Source address translation, automap, SNAT pools
- **Persistence** — Cookie, source IP, SSL session, universal, hash
- **High Availability** — Active-active, active-standby, config sync, failover
