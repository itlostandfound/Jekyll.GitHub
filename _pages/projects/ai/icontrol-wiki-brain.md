## iControl LLM Wiki Brain

Based on Andrej Karpathy's "LLM Wiki", creating a full iControl knowledge base for all known iControl methods (which will likely be converted to an MCP). Partially created from F5 documentation, but the ASM module — due to complexity — is not included. Using an agent taught to crawl a BIG-IP VE in the lab to discover, crawl, and document each undocumented endpoint.
