## Minotaur Needs a Mission — Asus Ascent GX10

"I need an AI Intervention", is what I told my Brother when I bought this :-)

He then asked "Why?" to which I responded "I already know how to use AI, work with Agents, create Agentic Workflow Applications, but that is all literally just 'using it' in one form or another.  I need to learn how to TEACH it too.  That combinded with having that much horsepower to run Local LLM's is not a bad thing.  We are in the 'token-o-plenty' times and then that era ends people are going to be in trouble...and I don't plan on being one of them."

The Minotaur has horsepower. The Asus Ascent GX10 is a compact AI workstation that needs a defined role in the lab — whether that's training, inference at scale, or something that hasn't been invented yet.
